Hypertext Transfer Protocol

Welcome

Hypertext Transfer Protocol is one of the most significant technologies developed for the internet. Without HTTP, the internet we know wouldn't be possible! This website is designed to explore the intricacies of HTTP and other related technologies. You'll find in-depth analyses of HTTP, along with more accessible overviews of topics like; HTML, the client-server model, the history of the internet and the World Wide Web, and much more! Welcome and enjoy the content!

Sir Tim Berners-Lee
